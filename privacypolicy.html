Cookies

We use Google Analytics to help improve and enhance user experience on our website. By using our website, you consent to the placement of cookies on your device.

Cookies are small, usually encrypted text files, which are used to perform various functions such as storing your preferences, allowing you to easily navigate between web pages, and improving the overall user experience. These cookies are located in browser directories and are useful since they have the ability to recognize a user’s PC or mobile device, and customize the user experience by transmitting small amounts of information back to the site. Cookies also help third party agencies to collect information about user profiles, their interactions, actions, and behavior, which help site owners to improve components and functionalities available on the website.

The functionalities of some of our cookies that are processed via third parties are limited to providing us with web analytics and more detailed site activity information and intelligence about our website. Information collected via cookies on our website is anonymous and helps us improve our customer service.

For instance, we analyze information pertaining to domains from where visitors come from, visitor activity, etc. We may use this information at an aggregate level to identify trends and statistics, which help improve the services we offer to our customers.
