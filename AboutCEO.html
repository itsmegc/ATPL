MR. MANOJ NAUDIYAL

Founder & CEO
Manoj is a product designer/marketer by profession and an entrepreneur at heart.
Ever since he started working he had this dream of taking entrepreneurship as his full-time career.
In the year 2015, after spending more than a decade in the corporate world, he decided to venture out of his own and chose his hometown Dehradun to start his entrepreneurial journey. Hence, he founded Arohar Technologies.

Manoj is a seasoned technologist with 17+ years of diverse IT experience. His corporate life includes working for about half a decade in the United States of America (USA), a couple of years in Australia, and a few years in Bangalore, Pune (India).
He is MS in Software Engineering from BITS Pilani and a TOGAF® 9 certified enterprise architect from The Open Group.

Manoj brings to the table a consulting acumen, hands-on knowledge of Business and IT operations, and learning from top multinational companies.
He is a person with a great ability to quickly build rapport with business and technology members.

In the second year of his entrepreneurship journey, on 1st July 2017, India switched to GST. The country witnessed the single biggest tax reform undertaken since independence. On the very same day, we launched ‘TaxSo'(SaaS-based GST compliant Accounting software) under his leadership. And since then team Arohar never looked back.
