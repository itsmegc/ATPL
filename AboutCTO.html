MR. SANDEEP KUNWAR

Co-Founder & CTO
Sandeep Kunwar is a technologist, tinkerer & traveller. He has almost 2 decades of IT industry experience under his belt.

At Arohar, he is responsible for outlining the technological vision, implementing technology strategies, overseeing the development and formulating solutions by converting ideas into realities.

A Globetrotter who has worked with top MNCs throughout the world enabling multiple end to end implementations. His experience includes working in Telecom, Healthcare, Insurance, Oil & Gas, Manufacturing verticals using a wide array of software landscapes. Before joining Arohar, Sandeep was working with IBM as Software Architect.

From the days of BASIC & DOS, he still enjoys being on top of cutting edge technologies and their application for the greater good.
