FEATURED PRODUCT - TaxSo ERP

TaxSo ERP is a SaaS (Software as a Service) based ERP application from Arohar Technologies Pvt Ltd. TaxSo ERP brings simplification, access and affordability together to enable businesses to plan, track and manage daily activities involving supply chain, manufacturing, services, financials, human resources and other processes.

Features Of TaxSo

TaxSo is a SaaS (Software as a Service) based GST Compliant business management software solution from Arohar Technologies Pvt Ltd. It provides a reliable and efficient way to manage your inventory and accounting requirements.

1

Purchasing

  • Create / Manage Purchase Orders
  • Make Goods Received Notes(GRNs)
  • Create / Manage Debit Notes
  • Track / View / Manage Vendor Bills

2

Sales Module

  • Create / Manage Sales Orders
  • Create / Manage Quotations
  • Generate Invoice PDFs
  • Send Quotation Email To Customers

3

Inventory

  • Add / Edit New Products
  • Manufacturer Wise Report
  • Product Wise Stock Register
  • Generate Barcodes for Products

4

Invoicing

  • Generate Invoice PDFs

  • Settle Customer / Supplier Payments

General Reports

  • Track Payment Records
